🎯 Our Mission

This project — AI Based Early Human Skin Disease Prediction Using Chatbot — is an educational tool designed to make preliminary skin disease screening accessible to everyone. Using advanced deep learning, the chatbot analyzes skin images and provides instant screening results for 11 skin conditions.

⚙️ How It Works

📸
Image Upload

You upload a clear photograph of the affected skin area through the secure interface.

🧠
AI Analysis

Our EfficientNetB0 neural network processes the image, comparing it against patterns learned from thousands of dermatological images.

📊
Results & Info

You receive a prediction with confidence scores, probability distribution, risk assessment, and detailed disease information.

🛠️ Technology Stack

🧠 TensorFlow Deep Learning Framework
🏗️ EfficientNetB0 Pre-trained CNN Model
🌐 Flask Python Web Framework
📈 Chart.js Data Visualization

🦠 Supported Conditions (11 Classes)

The AI model is trained on and can screen for the following 11 skin conditions:

🔴 Acne 🔴 Eczema 🐛 Infestations & Bites 🦋 Lupus ⚠️ Melanoma 🟠 Psoriasis 🔴 Rosacea 🍄 Tinea (Ringworm) ✅ Unknown / Normal ⚪ Vitiligo 🟡 Warts & Molluscum

📊 Model Accuracy

The model uses transfer learning with EfficientNetB0 pre-trained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on a curated dermatological dataset of 11 skin conditions. The two-phase training approach — head training followed by fine-tuning — targets 70–80% validation accuracy across all disease classes.

Accuracy varies by condition and image quality. The model performs best with well-lit, focused images of the affected area.

⚠️ Important Medical Disclaimer: This AI-based chatbot is an educational tool and does NOT provide medical diagnoses. Results should not be used as a substitute for professional medical evaluation. Always consult a certified dermatologist or healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and treatment. If you notice any concerning skin changes, seek professional medical attention promptly.
